6.Salmon in the oven

Ingredients

Essential items:
  • 1 fillet of salmon, skinned (tails are cheapest)
  • Any veggies you like: tomatoe, peppers, zucchinis, etc
  • Liquid cream
  • Black pepper
  • Salt

    • Method

    1. Put the salmon in an oven dish - add chopped veggies and cream on top-with salt and pepper.
    2. Grill on a high heat for about 25mins. If it is thick fillet, it will take a little longer to cook through, always check if the middle if cooked.
    3. You can serve it with rice or pasta.
