5.Vegetarian Rice

Ingredients

Essential items:
  • 1/2 chopped onion
  • 1 chopped garlic clove
  • 1 chopped courgette
  • 1 grated carrot
  • 1 chopped red or yellow pepper
  • 75g frozen peas
  • 1/2 tin of chopped tomatoes
  • 350ml Vegetable stock
  • 150g Dried brown rice
  • 1 tbsp of dried parsley
  • 1 tsp tumeric
  • Seasoning

    • Method

    Serves 2: Prep time 10mins: cook time 25mins
    1. Add onion, garlic and turmeric to pan with a splash of veg stocks.
    2. Stirring, cook gently for a 3mins then add the chopped courgette, grated carrot and chopped pepper
    3. Stir well for a further 3mins then add the dried rice
    4. Stir rice in well with the vegetables
    5. Once rice os disturbed well throughout the mixture add the stock- leave to boil
    6. Once boiled immediately turn heat down to simmer and cover. Cook for 10mins
    7. Add in chopped tomatoes and frozed peas and stir
    8. Cover and simmer for further 10mins
    9. Once cooked stir in dried parsely and with seasoning
    10. Spoon rice into 2 dishes
